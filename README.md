Project Name: AI Health Risk Mini Predictor

Team Name: Hacktivators

Problem Statement

Access to preventive healthcare remains limited, especially in rural and semi-urban areas where early diagnosis tools are not readily available. Many individuals ignore early symptoms due to lack of awareness or limited access to medical professionals, which can lead to serious health complications.

To address this issue, we propose an AI Health Risk Mini Predictor, a system that analyzes user health information such as age, symptoms, lifestyle factors, and medical history to estimate potential health risks. The system provides early alerts and risk predictions, encouraging individuals to seek timely medical consultation before health conditions become severe.

Proposed Solution

We are implementing an AI-powered health risk prediction platform that helps individuals and households monitor potential health risks and encourages regular preventive healthcare practices.

The system provides separate user access for individuals within the same household, ensuring that each person can track their own health data privately while still being part of a shared household system.

The platform focuses primarily on AI-based risk prediction, which works at two levels:

Individual Health Risk Prediction:
The AI model analyzes personal data such as age, reported symptoms, and medical history to estimate potential health risks and provide an early warning score.

Household Health Risk Prediction:
Since many health conditions are influenced by shared environments, lifestyle habits, and genetic factors, the system also evaluates health trends within a household to detect common risk patterns and recommend preventive measures for the entire family.

Users can regularly log symptoms and update their health records, allowing the AI system to continuously update risk predictions and provide timely alerts when potential risks increase.

Innovation and Creativity

The solution includes several innovative features designed to encourage preventive healthcare and increase user engagement.

AI-Based Health Risk Prediction

The core innovation of the system is the AI health risk prediction model, which analyzes health-related inputs such as age, symptoms, and medical history to generate a health risk score for individuals. This allows users to understand potential health risks early and take preventive action.

The system also analyzes household-level health patterns, helping detect possible shared health risks within families.

Gamified Health Engagement and Rewards

To motivate users to regularly track their health, the platform introduces a reward-based gamification system.

Users earn reward points when they:

Log their symptoms

Update their health records

Complete regular health checkups

Follow preventive health recommendations

These reward points can later be redeemed for discounts on healthcare checkups, healthcare products, or essential medical services, encouraging continuous health monitoring.

Multilingual AI Chatbot Assistance

The platform includes an AI chatbot capable of communicating in multiple languages, allowing users from rural and semi-urban areas to easily interact with the system.

The chatbot assists users by:

Guiding them through symptom reporting

Explaining their health risk predictions

Informing them about reward points and health recommendations

Providing basic health awareness in local languages

This improves accessibility and ensures that language barriers do not prevent users from using the system.

Technical Complexity and Stack

Frontend:
React.js for building a dynamic and responsive user interface.

Backend:
Node.js and Express.js providing RESTful APIs to manage user data, health records, and risk prediction requests.

Database:
MongoDB with Mongoose for flexible storage of user health data and household health records.

AI / ML:
Machine learning models used to analyze user health inputs and generate health risk predictions for individuals and households.

Chatbot System:
Integration of an AI chatbot capable of interacting with users in multiple languages to improve accessibility.

Usability and Impact

Early Health Risk Detection:
The system helps individuals identify potential health risks early by analyzing symptoms and health history using AI.

Preventive Healthcare Encouragement:
By providing risk alerts and health recommendations, users are encouraged to consult healthcare professionals before conditions worsen.

Gamified Health Participation:
The reward-based system motivates users to consistently log their health information and participate in preventive healthcare practices.

Accessible Healthcare Guidance:
The multilingual AI chatbot ensures that users from rural communities can easily interact with the system and understand their health insights.
